What hardwood species work best for Papillion homes?

Oak is the most popular choice for Papillion homeowners due to its durability and classic grain pattern. Red oak and white oak both perform well in Nebraska's climate. Hickory is another excellent option for families with active kids or pets because of its superior hardness. We also install maple, walnut, and ash. During your free consultation, we bring samples so you can see each species in your home's lighting.
