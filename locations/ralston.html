How do I maintain my hardwood floors after refinishing in Ralston?

After refinishing, avoid walking on the floors for at least 24 hours and wait 72 hours before placing furniture back. For ongoing care, sweep or vacuum regularly with a hardwood-safe attachment, clean with a damp mop using pH-neutral hardwood floor cleaner, and use felt pads under all furniture legs. In Ralston's climate, maintain indoor humidity between 35 and 55 percent year-round to prevent wood movement. We provide a detailed care guide with every project.
