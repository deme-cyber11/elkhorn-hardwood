Hardwood Floor Refinishing in Millard

North Elkhorn' premier planned community deserves hardwood floors that match the quality of its family-focused neighborhoods.

Millard's Family Homes & Modern Floors

Millard is one of Elkhorn' most desirable family neighborhoods, stretching across the northern corridor with master-planned subdivisions, top-rated schools, and easy access to shopping and dining along Millard Parkway. Homes here span from the early 1990s through brand-new construction, with the majority built between 2000 and 2015 — a period when builder-grade hardwood was standard in living rooms, dining areas, and hallways.

While these floors were well-built, many Millard homes now have 10–20 years of family life showing on their hardwood: kid-related scratches, pet wear patterns in high-traffic corridors, and the telltale golden-orange stain that dates a home instantly. The good news? These floors have plenty of wood thickness remaining and are perfect candidates for a complete color transformation through refinishing.

Common Flooring Challenges in Millard Homes

  • Dated builder stain colors: The golden oak and honey tones popular in early 2000s construction now look outdated. A modern stain color like warm walnut or weathered gray can take 10 years off your home's appearance.
  • High-traffic wear patterns: Open floor plans common in Millard mean long sight lines where wear patterns are clearly visible — especially the paths between kitchen, living room, and garage entry.
  • Pet damage from family pets: Millard is a pet-friendly community, and years of dog nails on hardwood create surface scratches that dull the finish and expose bare wood to moisture.
  • Preparation for resale: Millard homes move quickly in the Elkhorn market. Refinished hardwood floors are one of the highest-ROI improvements you can make before listing — our clients report $8,000–$15,000 in added home value from a $3,000–$5,000 refinishing project.
