About Us — Elkhorn Hardwood Floor Specialists

Locally owned. Douglas County based. Refinishing and restoring hardwood floors across Elkhorn, Delavan, Lake Geneva, Burlington, and surrounding communities.

Our Story

Why We Started Elkhorn Hardwood

Most Elkhorn homeowners dealing with worn hardwood floors have had the same experience: a contractor who quotes low, moves fast, and leaves a finish that starts peeling or gapping within a year. Douglas County's climate — cold dry winters that pull moisture from wood, humid summers that push it back — demands a different approach than a cookie-cutter refinishing job. We built this business around a simpler idea: do the work correctly for Nebraska's conditions, price it honestly, and treat every floor like it's in our own home.

We're not the cheapest option in the county. We are the option that gets the finish right the first time, uses commercial-grade materials appropriate for Nebraska's seasonal swings, and doesn't disappear once the check clears.

What We Stand For

What Makes Us Different

Three things homeowners tell us matter most after they've worked with us:

Written fixed-price quotes

The price we quote is the price you pay. Every job gets an itemized written estimate before work starts. If we find something unexpected once we start — a subfloor issue, boards that are thinner than they look — we stop, show you what we found, and discuss options before any additional work proceeds. No surprise invoices.

We call back within 1 hour

We answer the phone or call back within an hour during business hours — no exceptions. Homeowners shopping for floor work shouldn't have to leave three voicemails and wait three days for a response. If you call and we're on a job, you'll hear from us before the day is out.

Nebraska climate expertise

We understand what Douglas County's winters do to hardwood floors. The contraction from forced-air heat, the expansion in summer humidity, the finish systems that hold up to it and the ones that don't. Our process — acclimation times, finish selection, moisture readings — is calibrated for this specific climate, not for a mild market where these variables don't matter.

Our Scope

What We Do

We specialize in hardwood floor refinishing, installation, and repair for homeowners and businesses in Elkhorn, Delavan, Lake Geneva, Burlington, Williams Bay, Twin Lakes, Whitewater, and surrounding Douglas County communities. Our core services are hardwood refinishing, new hardwood installation, and floor repair and board replacement. We don't do carpet, LVT, or tile — hardwood is all we do, which means we're better at it.

Coverage

Who We Serve

We serve homeowners and businesses across Douglas County, Nebraska — including Elkhorn, Delavan, Lake Geneva, Burlington, Williams Bay, Twin Lakes, Whitewater, Papillion, Darien, and Walworth. Most of our work is within a 30-mile radius of Elkhorn. If you're outside that range, call us — we may still be able to help depending on job size and scheduling.

Our Promise

Our Commitment

Every job gets a written quote before any work starts. If we find something we didn't expect once the project is underway, we stop and show you before any additional work proceeds. The price we quote is the price you pay. We leave the job clean and we give you the care instructions you need to protect the investment — in writing, before we leave the site.
