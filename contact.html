What Is Hardwood Floor Installation and Refinishing?

Hardwood floor installation involves the precise laying of new wood flooring planks directly onto a prepared subfloor. This process begins with thorough subfloor inspection and preparation, including ensuring it is level, dry, and structurally sound, sometimes incorporating a moisture barrier. We then install your chosen hardwood – be it solid wood like oak, maple, or hickory, or engineered planks – using methods such as nail-down, glue-down, or floating techniques, depending on the wood type and subfloor. Refinishing, conversely, is the restoration of existing hardwood floors. It entails sanding away the old finish and minor imperfections through multiple passes with progressively finer grits, often employing dustless sanding systems for a cleaner process. Following sanding, we can repair minor damage, apply a new stain to change the color if desired, and then seal the wood with multiple coats of a durable protective finish, such as polyurethane (water or oil-based) or penetrating oils, to restore its luster and protect it from future wear.

Common Questions About Hardwood Floor Services

How much does Hardwood Floor Services cost in Elkhorn, NE (Milwaukee area)?

The cost for hardwood floor services in Elkhorn, NE, varies. New installation typically ranges from $8 to $15+ per square foot, including materials and labor, depending on the wood species, complexity, and subfloor conditions. Refinishing services usually fall between $3 and $6 per square foot, influenced by the existing floor's condition, the number of sanding passes required, and the type of finish chosen. These prices are competitive for the Milwaukee area market.

How long does Hardwood Floor Services take?

The duration of hardwood floor projects depends on the scope. A typical new hardwood floor installation for an average-sized home (around 1,000 sq ft) usually takes between 3 to 7 days, including preparation and finishing. Hardwood floor refinishing, which involves sanding, staining, and multiple coats of finish, generally takes 2 to 5 days for a similar area, primarily due to drying times between finish coats.

How do I know if I need Hardwood Floor Services?

Several signs indicate your hardwood floors might need professional attention. These include visible deep scratches, scuffs, and gouges that penetrate the finish, dull or faded areas where the protective layer has worn thin, water damage causing discoloration or warping, or uneven staining from previous DIY attempts. If your floors look perpetually dirty even after cleaning, feel rough to the touch, or if you simply desire a fresh look or updated color, it's time to consider our services.
