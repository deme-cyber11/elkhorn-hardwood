What to Expect After You Reach Out

Our estimate process is designed to be quick, thorough, and completely pressure-free.

Step 1: Quick Conversation

We'll call or email you within 24 hours to learn about your project — floor type, approximate size, what you're looking for, and your timeline. This takes about 5 minutes.

Step 2: On-Site Assessment

We visit your Colorado Springs home, measure the space, check floor condition and thickness, identify any repairs needed, and discuss finish options. Takes about 20–30 minutes. No pressure, no commitment.

Step 3: Detailed Written Estimate

You receive a clear, itemized estimate with no hidden fees. We break down every cost — sanding, staining, finish, repairs — so you know exactly what you're paying for. Valid for 30 days.

Step 4: Schedule at Your Convenience

If you'd like to proceed, we schedule the project at a time that works for you. Most projects can begin within 1–2 weeks of approval, depending on the season.

Prefer to Talk? Call Us Now.

Our team is available Monday through Saturday to answer questions, discuss your project, or schedule your free on-site estimate. No voicemail — a real person answers during business hours.
