The Process

What Refinishing Actually
Looks Like — Start to Finish

From the estimate to the final coat, here's what to expect when you book with Elkhorn Hardwood.

1

Free In-Home Estimate

We assess floor condition, measure the space, note repairs needed, and give you written pricing. Fixed per-sq-ft — you know the number before work starts.

2

Repairs & Floor Prep

Board replacement, gap filling, squeaky subfloor fastening — all prep work happens before sanding. A refinish is only as good as what's underneath.

3

Dustless Sanding

HEPA-filtered equipment sands to bare wood across multiple grits. If you're doing a color change, test patches go on here for your approval before we proceed.

4

Staining (if applicable)

Stain is applied and sealed. Oil-based stains need 24-hour dry time — we schedule around Nebraska's seasonal conditions, not a generic timeline.

5

Finish Coats & Cure

2–3 coats of Bona Mega ONE, lightly buffed between coats. Light traffic in 24 hours; furniture back in 72; area rugs after 30 days. Backed by a 3-year warranty.
